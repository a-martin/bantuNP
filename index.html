Symposium on the Syntax of the Bantu Noun Phrase

Welcome!

The Symposium on the Syntax of the Bantu Noun Phrase is a one-day event bringing together linguists from a range of backgrounds to discuss the syntax of the Bantu NP, with a special focus on word order variation. It will take place on September 6th, 2018 at Queen Mary University of London.

Registration

If you would like to register to attend the symposium (free of charge), please sign up on our Eventbrite page.

Invited speakers

Three invited speakers will give talks followed by discussion sessions.

Vicki Carstens

Professor at Southern Illinois University specialising in African languages and generativist syntax

Peter Muriungi

Associate professor of Linguistics at Chuka University specialising in the syntax of Bantu languages

Lutz Marten

Professor at the Scool of Oriental and African Studies at the University of London specialising in African linguistics
